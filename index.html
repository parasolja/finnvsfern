Finn vs. Fern Game

Welcome to Finn vs Fern Game!

Meet the characters

Finn - is the main protagonist of the series Adventure Time. Finn is a 5-foot tall teenage human with a beutiful blond hair.

Fern - was the result of Finn's Grass Sword and Finn Sword merging into one being. And he's a major antagonist.

Weapons & Hay Boxes

Every character is able to pick up one of the available weapons. The weapons are randomly located all over the map. After picking up a new weapon, the previous weapon will b left behind. The default weapon is hands, they can cause 10 points damage.

- Grass Sword. Can cause 20 points damage.

- Finn Sword. Can cause 20 points damage.

- Scarlet Sword. Can cause 20 points damage.

- Demon Sword. Can cause 20 points damage.

- Magic Wand. Can cause 30 points damage.

- Mushroom Bomb. Can cause 40 points damage.

- the characters can't walk on hay boxes

Rules

1. Every character is able to move three steps horizontally or vertically at a time. To see whose turn is next, just look on the very top of the game screen.

2. The blocking mode reduces the damage to 50%, but also does the same with the attack power.

3. As soon as one of the character's health bar is reached 0 the game is over, to play again please refresh the page and enjoy!.
